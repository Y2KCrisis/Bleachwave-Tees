Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a custom order?

Just head to our Contact page and send a description of your design idea. We'll get back to you to confirm and start the process!

Can I see examples before I order?

Yes! Check out the Gallery page for designs we've made in the past. You can also request something similar.

Do you ship internationally?

At this time, we only ship within the United States. Stay tuned for international shipping updates.

How long does it take to make a shirt?

Most shirts are made within 3–5 business days after we confirm the order, depending on the design complexity.

What sizes are available?

We carry sizes from XS to XXL. If you're unsure, feel free to reach out before placing your order!
